In the heart of the ancient forest, sunlight barely touched the ground, and shadows moved like living creatures. Birds sang melodies unknown to humans, while the wind whispered secrets of forgotten times. Travelers who dared enter often felt a strange pull, as if the forest itself was alive and observing them. Legends told of hidden paths, enchanted streams, and creatures that could change shape at will. Curiosity and caution walked hand in hand here, shaping every careful step and thought.
